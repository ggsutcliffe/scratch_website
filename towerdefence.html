tower defence

how to play

Open the shop in the top right corner buy turrets from the shop with cash use cash to

upgrade turrets earn enough cash to buy undiscovered turrets to discover them click the

next wave button in the top left corner to start the next wave defend against the

approaching army of enemies earn cash by defeating each enemy don't let them reach the

other side go for as long as you can.
