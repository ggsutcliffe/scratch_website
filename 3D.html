--------------------------CONTROLS----------------------------------

-up arrow to move forward

-right & left arrows to turn

-down arrow to turn around

-A&D to side step

-W is also forward

-D is backward

-R is respawn

-M is toggle map

-C&V to hide and show sliders

------------------------HOW TO PLAY--------------------------------

-run around and collect as many dimonds as you can before the bad guys get you
